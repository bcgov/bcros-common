Paying Exact Amount

Please ensure that you pay the exact amount stated on your monthly statement. Over or underpayments may lead to service interruptions and processing delays.

For every payment, please send an email with the following details after your payment to ensure the proper processing of payment and to prevent any delays.

Email address: BCROSRev@gov.bc.ca

Subject Line: EFT/Wire transfer Payment - Your BC Registries and Online Services Account Name - Account Number

Include the following details in your email:

  • BC Registries and Online Services account name (and branch name if applicable)
  • BC Registries and Online Services account number (located on your account information page)
  • Date of payment
  • Total amount of payment
  • Short name (this is provided by your bank with every outgoing payment)
  • Statement number (located on the statement PDF for the statement that you are paying)
  • If you are paying for multiple BC Registries and Online Services accounts, include all relevant information clearly for each account.

Bank Charges

Your financial institution may have standard charges that apply to EFT or wire transfer payments. Ensure your financial institution does not deduct charges from the payment amount due as this will result in an underpayment.

Refund Process

In the event of an overpayment, a refund will be issued after staff approval. Please follow these steps to set up direct deposit for potential refunds:

  1. Download and fill out the direct deposit application form
  2. Print the completed form
  3. Attach a void cheque to the form
  4. Mail both items to:

Ministry of Citizens' Services, Service BC, Finance

PO Box 9804 Stn Prov Govt

Victoria BC V8W 9W1

Setup may take up to 30 days. Approved refunds will be deposited to the bank account provided in your application.

Bank Account Changes
