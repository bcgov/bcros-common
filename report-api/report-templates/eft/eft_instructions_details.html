Statement and Due Date

Statements are issued on the 1st day of each month, covering all transactions from the previous calendar month. The statement will display a payment due date (the month's last day), by which you must settle the outstanding balance.

To view your statement, log in to the statement page. A statement notification email will be sent on the statement issue date. Please ensure notifications and recipient settings are enabled in your account settings.

Paying Exact Amount

Please ensure that you pay the exact amount stated on your monthly statement. Over or underpayments may lead to service interruptions and processing delays.

Refund Process

In order to receive a refund of any sort, follow these steps to set up direct deposit for potential refunds:

  1. Download and fill out the direct deposit application form
  2. Print the completed form
  3. Attach a void cheque to the form
  4. Mail both items to:

Ministry of Citizens' Services, Service BC, Finance
PO Box 9804 Stn Prov Govt
Victoria BC V8W 9W1

Setup may take up to 30 days, so start the process early to ensure timely refunds. Approved refunds will be deposited to the bank account provided in your application.
